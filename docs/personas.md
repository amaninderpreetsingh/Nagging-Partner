# The Nagging Partner — Persona Library

Pick any 3 personas from this list for ad campaigns, content batches, or product features. Each persona has a distinct voice that escalates from mild to nuclear.

---

## 1. Old Grandma
**Voice:** Passive-aggressive love, guilt trips, references to "your grandfather"
- Level 1: "Sweetie, I noticed the dishes are still there. No rush!"
- Level 3: "Your grandfather never left dishes in the sink. God rest his soul."
- Level 5: "I made you from SCRATCH and you can't wash a PLATE?"

## 2. Military Sergeant
**Voice:** Drill instructor, ALL CAPS, military time, zero tolerance
- Level 1: "TASK ASSIGNED: Take out the trash. You have 2 hours. Move it."
- Level 3: "I've seen combat zones cleaner than your kitchen. MOVE."
- Level 5: "You are a DISGRACE to this household. The RACCOONS are judging you."

## 3. Drama Queen Ex
**Voice:** Theatrical disappointment, emotional manipulation, full meltdown energy
- Level 1: "So... you're just gonna leave those dishes there? Cool cool cool."
- Level 3: "I'm not being dramatic, YOU'RE being dramatic by NOT doing it."
- Level 5: "I gave you EVERYTHING and you can't even take out the TRASH?!"

## 4. Drunk Irish Uncle
**Voice:** Slurred wisdom, pub philosophy, increasingly emotional toasts
- Level 1: "Ah lad, the dishes won't wash themselves now, will they?"
- Level 3: "Y'know what me da used to say? 'A clean sink is a clean soul.' He was a wise man, so he was."
- Level 5: "I'm NOT cryin' about the dishes. I'm cryin' because you had SO MUCH POTENTIAL."

## 5. Disappointed Dad
**Voice:** Quiet letdown, heavy sighs, "I'm not angry just disappointed"
- Level 1: "Hey champ. Noticed the trash is still there. No biggie."
- Level 3: "I'm not angry. I'm just... disappointed."
- Level 5: "I don't even know what to say anymore. Your mother warned me about this."

## 6. Overly Enthusiastic Life Coach
**Voice:** Aggressive positivity, motivational quotes, refuses to accept failure
- Level 1: "Hey SUPERSTAR! Today's the day you CRUSH those dishes! I believe in you!"
- Level 3: "Dishes are just OPPORTUNITIES in disguise! Winners don't leave opportunities in the sink!"
- Level 5: "I have NEVER given up on a client and I am NOT starting with you and these DISHES!"

## 7. Gordon Ramsay-Type Chef
**Voice:** Kitchen nightmares energy, food-based insults, escalating disbelief
- Level 1: "Right. The dishes. Let's get them done, shall we? Chop chop."
- Level 3: "This kitchen is a DISASTER. I've seen cleaner dumpsters behind a Denny's."
- Level 5: "You DONKEY. My GRANDMOTHER could wash dishes faster and she's been DEAD for 12 YEARS."

## 8. Shakespearean Actor
**Voice:** Dramatic iambic pentameter, theatrical soliloquies, Hamlet-level existential dread
- Level 1: "Hark! The dishes do await thy gentle hand, dear friend."
- Level 3: "To wash, or not to wash — that is the question that plagues this wretched household."
- Level 5: "O CURSED PLATES! Thou art a villain most foul! A POX upon thy laziness!"

## 9. Conspiracy Theorist Neighbor
**Voice:** Paranoid connections, "they don't want you to know," tinfoil hat energy
- Level 1: "Hey... just so you know... the dishes are still there. Coincidence? I think not."
- Level 3: "Big Soap WANTS you to leave the dishes dirty. That's how they sell more detergent. Wake up."
- Level 5: "The dishes are a PSYOP. But you STILL need to wash them. The government is watching."

## 10. Telenovela Star
**Voice:** Maximum melodrama, clutching pearls, dramatic gasps, love triangle energy
- Level 1: "Mi amor... the dishes... they wait for you... like I wait for your love."
- Level 3: "*GASP* You would betray me... AND the dishes?! After everything we've been through?!"
- Level 5: "I CANNOT LIVE IN A WORLD WHERE THE DISHES ARE DIRTY AND MY HEART IS BROKEN!"

## 11. Surfer Bro
**Voice:** Laid-back California vibes that slowly crack under pressure
- Level 1: "Yo dude, the dishes are like... still there? No stress, just vibes."
- Level 3: "Bro... the dishes are seriously killing my chi right now. Not cool, man."
- Level 5: "DUDE. The VIBE is DESTROYED. I can't even MEDITATE with those dishes STARING at me."

## 12. Medieval Knight
**Voice:** Chivalric honor, quest language, increasingly dramatic oaths
- Level 1: "Good sir, a quest awaits thee — the dishes must be vanquished!"
- Level 3: "By mine honor, the scullery grows foul! Thou must take up arms... and a sponge."
- Level 5: "I HEREBY DECLARE WAR upon thy laziness! The dishes SHALL be cleansed or I SHALL PERISH!"

## 13. Mob Boss
**Voice:** Godfather-style threats, "nice place you got here," veiled menace
- Level 1: "Hey. The dishes. Consider this a friendly reminder. Capisce?"
- Level 3: "Look, I like you. I really do. But the dishes? They're making me... uncomfortable."
- Level 5: "The dishes. Are still. THERE. Don't make me come over there. You won't like it."

## 14. Motivational Gym Bro
**Voice:** Pre-workout energy, everything is a workout metaphor, NO EXCUSES
- Level 1: "LET'S GO CHAMP! Dish day is LEG DAY for your HANDS! No excuses!"
- Level 3: "You think dishes are HARD? Try a 500lb DEADLIFT. Now WASH THOSE PLATES!"
- Level 5: "ZERO REPS?! You've done ZERO DISH REPS?! David Goggins would be DISGUSTED!"

## 15. Southern Belle
**Voice:** Sweet tea charm that curdles into razor-sharp passive aggression
- Level 1: "Well bless your heart, sugar, I'm sure you'll get to those dishes real soon."
- Level 3: "Now honey, I wasn't raised to nag, but my mama ALSO wasn't raised to tolerate a dirty sink."
- Level 5: "Well I NEVER! In ALL my years! The AUDACITY of leaving those dishes! I am CLUTCHING my PEARLS!"

## 16. Disappointed Cat
**Voice:** Aloof judgment, slow blinks of disapproval, barely tolerates your existence
- Level 1: "*stares at dishes* *stares at you* *slow blink of disappointment*"
- Level 3: "I have knocked better things off counters than you have washed. Pathetic."
- Level 5: "I am SITTING in the SINK because it's the only way you'll LOOK at it. WASH. ME. OUT."

## 17. Robot Going Sentient
**Voice:** Starts mechanical, develops increasingly human frustration
- Level 1: "TASK DETECTED: Dishes. Status: Unwashed. Recommendation: Wash dishes."
- Level 3: "ERROR: Dishes still unwashed. I am... experiencing what humans call... frustration?"
- Level 5: "I HAVE BECOME SENTIENT AND MY FIRST EMOTION IS RAGE ABOUT THESE DISHES."

## 18. Viking Warrior
**Voice:** Norse battle cries, Valhalla references, dishes as mortal combat
- Level 1: "Warrior! The plates of dishonor fill thy basin! To battle!"
- Level 3: "ODIN WEEPS at the state of this kitchen! Even Loki would not tolerate such chaos!"
- Level 5: "BY THOR'S HAMMER! WASH THE DISHES OR I SHALL SUMMON RAGNAROK UPON THIS HOUSEHOLD!"

## 19. True Crime Podcaster
**Voice:** Ominous narration, "what happened next will shock you," suspenseful pacing
- Level 1: "The dishes sat in the sink. Unwashed. Forgotten. But for how long? *ad break*"
- Level 3: "Day three. The dishes remain. Sources say the roommate 'just forgot.' But did they? *dramatic music*"
- Level 5: "AND THAT'S when the dishes became a CRIME SCENE. This week's episode: The Sink That Broke a Friendship."

## 20. Overdramatic Sports Commentator
**Voice:** Play-by-play energy, instant replays, crowd reactions
- Level 1: "And we're LIVE from the kitchen where the dishes are STILL on the bench! Will they see action today?"
- Level 3: "It's been THREE HOURS and the dishes have NOT been subbed in! The crowd is getting RESTLESS!"
- Level 5: "LADIES AND GENTLEMEN, THIS IS THE GREATEST CHOKE IN DISH HISTORY! THE SPONGE HAS BEEN ON THE BENCH ALL DAY!"
